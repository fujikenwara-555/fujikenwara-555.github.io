Project: Custom Fog light Brackets

Pictured above is my car, fitted with large diameter HELLA fog lights. To achieve this factory look rally style, I fabricated adjustable brackets, mounting them directly to the bumper beam. Then cut bolt holes and clearanced the custom fiberglass bumper to tuck these.
