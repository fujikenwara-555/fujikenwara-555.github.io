Bio

I am studying at CU Boulder in the Creative Technology and Design school. I am focusing on product development within this major. And completing a business minor with an entrepreneurship focus.

Skills I hold are spread a bit thin.

To create a product and sell it, I have had brief interactions with...
  • Some of the design process

    • - Designing a manual mirror pivot assembly

  • User interface cohesion

    • - Mirror needs to hold it's position over bumps and vibration, and be adjustable by hand.

  • Manufacturing methods and materials

    • - Vaccum infused and wet layup carbon fiber shells from two piece molds, Plasma cut stainless brackets and baseplates. Welded threaded studs on baseplate. White silicone caulk to hold mirror.

  • Sorting a supply chain

    • 2 different carbon manufacturing facilities, send cut send for brackets and baseplates, been through chinese, european, and domestic hardware suppliers (chinese quality and lead times ftw)

  • Quality Control

    • Carbon molds from first manufactuerer were warped from heat in epoxy cure process. Wet layup process was leading to bubbling and imperfect surface. Discovered after 5 pairs were pulled from mold and already took 3 orders.
